# Hello, Reader

This is my first blog post on my very own website — and it feels a little surreal to be writing it.

Friends have told me for a while that I have a lot to share and should start creating content. When they say _content_, they mean Instagram videos or YouTube. And honestly, I considered it. I even came close to starting a YouTube channel. But there are so many moving parts to making videos — editing, lighting, all the stuff I'm just not good at — that I never actually got started.

Then, while I was on a trip to Thailand, I wanted to try out Claude Opus 4.7, which had launched that very day. I didn't have my laptop with me, and somehow that constraint unlocked the idea: a blogging website. Writing on a phone is something I could actually do. It solved two problems at once, so I built it — literally on my phone, on a ferry ride to an island. I think that's a pretty solid origin story.

---

There's something I've come to accept about myself (my fiancée has been very vocal about it too): I get excited about something, dive in, and then suddenly lose interest. I'm aware of the pattern. I'm not promising I've broken it. But I'm hoping this space is different — somewhere I keep coming back to, because there's always something worth writing about.

What I want this to be is simple: a place where I share the things rattling around in my head. The tools I build. Things I've learned. Opinions I can't stop having. Books I read. The occasional rant. Whatever falls in between.

Here's what you can expect to find here:

- **Tech tools I build** — projects, experiments, things I'm proud of
- **Things I've learned recently** — breakdowns of topics I've gone deep on
- **General thoughts** — on anything, really
- **Book reviews & recommendations** — what I'm reading and whether it's worth your time
- **Life rants** — because sometimes you just need to say it
- **Everything in between** — the stuff that doesn't fit a category

---

Thanks for reading this far. I hope something here adds a little something to your life.

Let's begin.
